About Us

Vrishabha Properties & Constructions is a real estate and construction proprietorship based in Hassan, founded and operated by a single owner with a vision to provide transparent, reliable, and guided property services.

The business is associated with Boovanahalli Kantharaju Legacy, a real estate brand focused on development, realty services, entrepreneurship, and investment activities.

Established in June 2025, Vrishabha has already supported 100+ happy clients, helping buyers, sellers, and investors make confident property decisions in and around Hassan.

We are registered under the Ministry of MSME, Government of India, reflecting our commitment to ethical, transparent, and well-documented real estate practices.

  • Residential & commercial property sales
  • Farm land and investment property guidance
  • Joint development (JV) coordination
  • Layout development support
  • Construction guidance & consulting

Commission Policy

Vrishabha Properties & Constructions works on a professional brokerage and facilitation basis. A standard commission is applicable on every successful transaction.

Commission percentage or fixed charges may vary depending on property type (residential, commercial, land, or revenue property) and deal complexity. The exact commission amount will be mutually discussed and agreed before closing any deal.

Commission becomes payable once an agreement, advance, or final sale is completed between buyer and seller. The full commission amount must be cleared at least one day before property registration or on/before the day of registration. Site visits, negotiations, documentation assistance, and coordination are included as part of our service.

If a client attempts to directly complete a transaction with the property owner for any property introduced by Vrishabha Properties & Constructions, without our knowledge or involvement, the agreed commission remains fully applicable and must be settled, as our services and introductions form the basis of the transaction.

Role of Vrishabha Properties

Vrishabha Properties & Constructions acts as a property facilitator and coordinator. Final legal verification, documentation, and purchase responsibility rests solely with buyers and sellers.
